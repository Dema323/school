This is Leuven

The largest population of students creates a young vibrant atmosphere and a pleasent bustle in historic Leuven. Thanks to its university, Leuven is an innovative and conveniently located centre of knowledge, just a stone's throw away from Brussels, the European capital, and Brussels airport, at the intersection of two European motorways.

However, Leuven is also a mecca for gourmets and tourists who want to relax. A city where culture and creativity rule. A shopping centre for demand shoppers.
