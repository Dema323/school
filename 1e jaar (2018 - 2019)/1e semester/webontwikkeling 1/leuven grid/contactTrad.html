Contact Leuven

Contact Leuven+ for a guided tour

Leuven+ is an association of enthusiastic, experienced guides, certified by the Flanders Tourism Board. Not only do we offer a vast array of standard walks. We also develop bespoke visits and tours made to measure specific target groups such as persons with disabilities and schools. If you want to see Leuven through the eyes of a historical figure, check out our 'Take a walk with ...' formula.

Leuven+ offers tours in Dutch, English, French, German and Spanish. Feel free to browse through our offer or contact us for a customised tour.
